# CasperSect AI Demo Video Script

## Target length

2-3 minutes.

## Scene 1: Problem

"Web3 users, DeFi protocols, and RWA platforms need fast ways to identify risky wallet behavior before they interact with an address. Today, most users only see raw transactions. CasperSect AI turns that into agentic security intelligence."

## Scene 2: Product Intro

"CasperSect AI is an AI-powered wallet risk agent built on Casper. It analyzes wallet activity, assigns a risk score, explains the reason, recommends next actions, and records verifiable scan evidence on Casper Testnet."

## Scene 3: Connect Wallet

Show Casper Wallet connection or paste a Casper testnet public key.

Narration:
"First, the user connects Casper Wallet or enters a wallet address to analyze."

## Scene 4: Run AI Scan

Click **Run AI Scan**.

Narration:
"The backend risk engine and AI agent review recent wallet behavior, counterparty labels, transaction concentration, and outbound flow patterns."

## Scene 5: Explain Results

Show risk score, category, flags, and recommendation.

Narration:
"The agent explains why this wallet was classified as elevated risk and recommends recording the scan on-chain for auditability."

## Scene 6: On-chain Recording

Show generated evidence hash and Casper runtime args.

Narration:
"CasperSect creates a deterministic evidence hash and submits it to the CasperSect Scan Registry smart contract on Casper Testnet. This gives the scan a verifiable on-chain trail."

## Scene 7: Hackathon Fit

"This project combines Agentic AI, DeFi/RWA security relevance, Casper wallet integration, Odra smart contracts, and a transaction-producing Casper Testnet component."

## Closing

"CasperSect AI helps make Casper a safer home for the next generation of agentic decentralized applications."
